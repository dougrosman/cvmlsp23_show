pix2pix trained Model, StyleGAN2 trained model


This StyleGAN interpolation is created by scraping through datasets of my life from the past 3 years and applying a canny edge filter to each photograph. The canny edge filters and photographs are fed into Pix2Pix and the results are overlaid over my drawings.

Just as events and preoccupations in my day-to-day life influence each drawing, small imperceptible details in the photos from my life inform the texture of the Pix2Pix output. The nature of the improvised hand drawn compositions combined with the unpredictable output from Pix2Pix compound into unstable, vibrant landscapes.
